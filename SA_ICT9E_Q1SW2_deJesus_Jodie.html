Noli Me Tangere Characters

ImageNameDescription
Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin Also known as simply Ibarra, he is the protagonist of this novel. He has just returned to the Philippines after studying in Europe for seven years. Ibarra is of tall stature, brown-skinned, with red flushing cheeks. He also comes from a wealthy family.
Don Rafael Ibarra He is Ibarra's father. He sent his son to study abroad because he felt that the education of the Philippines during their time was hindering his son from reaching his full potential. During Ibarra's time in Europe, he was jailed for the imputation of a tax collector's murder. Don Rafael died right before his release.
Tenyente Guevarra He is of Spanish descent, and a lieutenant of the Civil Guard. He admires and respects the Ibarras. Tenyente Guevarra is not afraid to speak his mind as he openly supports Ibarra in his persuit of reform.
Don Santiago de los Santos Called by others as Kapitan Tiago. He is another wealthy Filipino who keeps close ties to the nobility and the church for personal gain.
Padre Damaso Served San Diego's parish church for 20 years before being replaced by Padre Salvi, a younger priest. He yearns for authority and expects to be respected by those around him. He is snobbish, scandalous, and narcissistic.
